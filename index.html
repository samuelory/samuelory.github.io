About my Project Idea

My idea is to allow users to use their phones as the vibrating token thingy that food stalls
use to inform customers that their food is here. Since the vibrating token is no longer something
that the customers have to return, the customer is no longer expected to stay in the area after joining the queue.
As such, the customer can join the queue, then leave and come back when it his turn.
